SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Forms series with Eskebornite.

Composition deviates very little from ideal CuFeS2. Its structure can be regarded as a derivative of the sphalerite structure in which half the Zn is replaced by Cu and the other half by Fe. This leads to a

doubling of the unit cell.
