SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

This sample consists of goethite rich altered lower Cambrian Harkless phyllite involved in hydothermal alteration about 7.5 Ma in the now relict western center hydrothermal center at Cuprite, NV. The sample was collected in the central part of the western center along its western margin.

END_SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION.

SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

The spectrum has absorptions characteristic of goethite. There is a broad Fe band at 0.9770 microns. Kaolinite is evident from doublets near 1.4 and 2.2 microns.

See: Swayze, G.A., 1997, The hydrothermal and structural history of the Cuprite Mining District, southwestern Nevada: an integrated geological and geophysical approach: unpublished Ph.D. Dissertation, University of Colorado at Boulder, 399p.
