SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

The sample was created by growing ice on a 77K cold finger from water vapor from triply distilled H2O from Gary Olhoeft's lab, USGS, Denver. The sample should have very little contaminants; being essentially pure H2O.
Only the infrared region was measured because the visible detector could not be used with the measurement setup.

Measured at 77K.
